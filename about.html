About the Viasat Browser

At Viasat, we’re on a mission to connect the world by making the internet accessible, affordable, and secure to everyone, anywhere.

With this mission in mind, we created the Viasat Browser web browser.  Viasat Browser strives toward a goal of what we like to call Instant Internet. To understand this, imagine if loading a webpage was as instantaneous as changing the channel on your TV. To do this, Viasat Browser continuously learns from the crowdsourced data of its network in order to browse smarter and faster.

Don’t take out word for it, see for yourself how the speed of Viasat Browser compares.
